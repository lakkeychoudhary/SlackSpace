🤖How to Use SlackSpace

1

Join the Hack Club Slack

Go to hackclub.com/slack and sign up. This is where SlackSpace lives.

2

Open any channel or DM

Go to any channel like #general, #random, or DM the bot directly.

3

Type a command

Type /sp-ping and hit Enter. The bot responds instantly with your latency!

4

Try all 12 commands

Use /sp-help to see everything SlackSpace can do. Or jump straight to /sp-joke for a laugh!
